Bibliography

Clint Eastwood, born in 1930, is an American actor, filmmaker, and former mayor of Carmel-by-the-Sea. He gained fame through spaghetti westerns and his iconic "Man with No Name" character. Eastwood's career spans a wide range of genres, and he is known for his roles in films like "Dirty Harry" and "Unforgiven." He is also a highly regarded director, with notable films such as "Mystic River" and "American Sniper." Eastwood's contributions have earned him multiple awards, cementing his status as a legendary figure in cinema.
