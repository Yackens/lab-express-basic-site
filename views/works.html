Experience

Actor:

  1. "A Fistful of Dollars" (1964) - Directed by Sergio Leone, this spaghetti western marked Eastwood's breakthrough role as the iconic "Man with No Name."
  2. "Dirty Harry" (1971) - Eastwood portrayed the tough and unyielding detective Harry Callahan in this crime thriller, which became one of his most memorable roles.
  3. "Unforgiven" (1992) - Eastwood starred in and directed this western drama, winning him several awards, including the Academy Awards for Best Picture and Best Director.
  4. "Million Dollar Baby" (2004) - In addition to directing, Eastwood delivered a powerful performance as a boxing trainer in this critically acclaimed film, for which he won the Academy Awards for Best Director and Best Actor.

Director:

  1. "Play Misty for Me" (1971) - Eastwood made his directorial debut with this psychological thriller, in which he also played the lead role of a radio DJ stalked by an obsessive fan.
  2. "Unforgiven" (1992) - As mentioned earlier, Eastwood directed and starred in this revisionist western, which garnered critical acclaim for its exploration of the complexities of violence.
  3. "Mystic River" (2003) - Eastwood directed this crime drama, based on the novel by Dennis Lehane, which received widespread praise for its performances and storytelling.
  4. "American Sniper" (2014) - Eastwood directed this biographical war drama, based on the life of Chris Kyle, a Navy SEAL sniper. The film was a commercial success and received several award nominations.
  5. "Sully" (2016) - Eastwood directed this biographical drama about the "Miracle on the Hudson" emergency landing of US Airways Flight 1549, starring Tom Hanks as Captain Chesley Sullenberger.
